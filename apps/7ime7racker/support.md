# 7ime7racker Support

For support questions, use the developer contact information listed for 7ime7racker on the App Store or the contact method published by this repository owner.

## Privacy

Read the [7ime7racker Privacy Policy]({{ "/apps/7ime7racker/privacy/" | relative_url }}) for information about app data handling.

## Before Contacting Support

Please include:

- The app version.
- The platform version, such as iOS, iPadOS, or watchOS.
- A short description of what happened.
- Steps that reproduce the issue, if available.

Do not include private time entries, client names, invoices, addresses, coordinates, screenshots with personal data, backups, or other sensitive data in public GitHub issues or pull requests.
