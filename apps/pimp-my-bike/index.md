# Pimp My Bike

Pimp My Bike is a local-first bike dashboard, rear-view mirror, ride safety, and dashcam app for iPhone.

## Documents

- [Privacy policy]({{ "/apps/pimp-my-bike/privacy/" | relative_url }})
- [Support]({{ "/apps/pimp-my-bike/support/" | relative_url }})

## App Summary

- Use the iPhone camera as a rear-view mirror while riding.
- Track ride speed, distance, bike setup, maintenance, parked-bike location, and ride events.
- Use optional safety features such as dashcam clips, close-pass warnings, road-quality detection, route aids, music controls, and voice commands.
- Keep app data local by default, with optional exports, Photos saves, App Store purchases, Apple Maps, weather, and POI lookups.
