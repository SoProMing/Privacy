# Pimp My Bike Support

For support questions, use the developer contact information listed for Pimp My Bike on the App Store or the contact method published by this repository owner.

## Privacy

Read the [Pimp My Bike Privacy Policy]({{ "/apps/pimp-my-bike/privacy/" | relative_url }}) for information about app data handling.

## Before Contacting Support

Please include:

- The app version.
- The iOS version.
- The iPhone model.
- A short description of what happened.
- Steps that reproduce the issue, if available.
- Which feature was involved, such as camera mirror, dashcam clips, location, map, voice commands, music, purchases, road-quality detection, or exports.

Do not include private routes, addresses, coordinates, bike identifiers, dashcam clips, incident packages, screenshots with personal data, exports, or other sensitive data in public GitHub issues or pull requests.
